####基本配置
Community Edition of IntelliJ + IdeaVim

mac上，长按击键:

```
defaults write com.jetbrains.intellij.ce ApplePressAndHoldEnabled -bool false
```

###Keymap
基本所有的操作，都替我搞定了

| function | mac | win |
|---|---|---|
|resume                 | F8            |F8             |
|setp over              | F10           |F10            |
|setp into              | F11           |F11            |
|setp out               | Shift + F11   |Shift + F11    |
|setting                | Command + ,   |Alt + ,        |
|project setting        | Command + ;   |Alt + ;        |
